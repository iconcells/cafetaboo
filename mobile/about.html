About Us

Café Taboo set out to be an everyday neighborhood café bar in inner Mission since 2010. With roots in the San Francisco diverse culture, we strive to reflect the same organic vibe in our sf-style cali-mexi food and drinks, faithful to uncompromising taste and healthy eating.

Whether its the 30-ponder we roast to make our shaved turkey sandwiches, the fair-trade organic coffee beans we use for our lattes, the free-range chicken we season for our enchiladas, or even the local craft beer we serve, we make every effort to source the freshest ingredients from our local community.

From staple like bacon cheese burger, build-your-own tossed salad, to authentic baja chicken burrito, Café Taboo continues to blend our unique San Franciscan brand of diversity into every cup and every dish we serve to our customers.
